CarFolio

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 28, 2025

1. Overview

CarFolio ("we", "our", or "us") is a vehicle maintenance and tracking app developed by an independent developer. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use CarFolio on iOS and Android.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. CarFolio is designed to keep your data on your device whenever possible.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Account information: name and email address when you create an account via email, Google, or Apple Sign-In.
  • Vehicle data: brand, model, year, mileage, fuel records, maintenance history, and documents you add manually.
  • Trip data: GPS location data collected during drive tracking sessions (only when you enable GPS tracking).

Information collected automatically:

  • App settings and preferences stored locally on your device.
  • Authentication tokens managed by Firebase Authentication.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the CarFolio service.
  • To calculate maintenance reminders, fuel consumption, and vehicle health scores.
  • To authenticate your account securely via Firebase.
  • To enable cloud backup of your garage data (only if you sign in).
  • To send local push notifications for maintenance reminders (no remote push servers).

4. Data Storage

Local storage: Vehicle data, documents, photos, and fuel records are stored locally on your device using device storage. We do not upload your documents or photos to any server.

Cloud storage: If you create a CarFolio account, basic garage data (vehicle names, mileage, maintenance records) may be synced to Firebase Firestore to enable multi-device access. You can use CarFolio as a guest without any cloud storage.

5. Location Data

CarFolio requests location permission only for the GPS trip tracking feature. Location data is used solely to calculate trip distance, route, and speed. Location data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

You can disable location access at any time in your device settings.

6. Third-Party Services

CarFolio uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google LLC) – Authentication and optional cloud database. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Sign-In – Optional authentication method. Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In – Optional authentication method. Apple Privacy Policy

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with any other third parties.

7. In-App Purchases

CarFolio offers optional subscription plans (CarFolio+ and CarFolio Fleet) processed through the App Store (Apple) and Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment information. All payments are handled by Apple or Google.

8. Children's Privacy

CarFolio is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app from your device. If you have a CarFolio account, you can request account and data deletion by contacting us at canberk.bzkrt@gmail.com. We will process your request within 30 days.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Your continued use of CarFolio after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
